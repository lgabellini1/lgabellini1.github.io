Death Stranding Terrain Scan

Overview

This project explores photorealistic terrain generation inspired by Death Stranding. The focus was on capturing the mood and lighting using Unreal Engine 5’s Lumen and Nanite systems.

Technical Breakdown

  • Procedural landscape with multi-layer material blending
  • Dynamic fog and volumetric lighting setup
  • Custom shader for rock/moss transitions
  • Sequencer cinematic captures
